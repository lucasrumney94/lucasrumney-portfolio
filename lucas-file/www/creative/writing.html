Heartbeat

Will you let me hear your heart, beat?
Will I find, that I've been weak
when I wake to find
you've left me behind.

I set the stakes too high
and I can't find why
You've left me high and dry.

What did you want from me?
I had nothing left, baby.
You took my heart and my head,
and you made them yours to keep.

I'm a shapeless shadow
of what I used to be
without you next to me.

~~~

Invested in a voice that no longer seeks to speak,
willed to silence by the clock’s tide,
the machine’s leveled squeak.

~~~
